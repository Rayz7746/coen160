SCU Rideshare

Carpooling for SCU students, By SCU Students

Drivers

Sick of paying for 6 dollar gas? We got you.

All you have to do is sign up! Make sure to put down all of your preferences, then see who our matching algorithm find for you! You'll get a ton of potential matches, all of whom are SCU students, and a score assigned to each one. From there on, you pick who you want the most, and you guys will work it out from there.
